# Code of Conduct

DataClaw follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for impact.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on the work.
- Help maintain a productive, welcoming project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or ban participants who violate this code. For the first public release, report conduct concerns through the maintainer contact published on the repository profile or release page.
